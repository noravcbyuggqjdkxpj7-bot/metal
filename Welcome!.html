Frequently Asked Questions

How do the three phases work?

Phase 1: Submit 30+ approved images for $30 instant reward. Phase 2: Reach $100 sales for $20 bonus (6-month validity). Phase 3: Monthly rewards based on performance tiers.

When are rewards paid out?

Phase 1 rewards are instant upon approval. Phase 2 rewards are paid when milestone is reached. Phase 3 rewards are calculated monthly and paid by the 5th of the following month.

What counts towards the 500 image limit?

The limit includes all active images in your portfolio across all phases. You can remove or replace images at any time, but the total cannot exceed 500.

How long does Phase 2 validity last?

Phase 2 milestone reward is valid for 6 months from when you complete Phase 1. You must reach $100 in cumulative sales within this period to earn the $20 bonus.
